Complete Dentures

DENTURES Dentures today aren’t like the false teeth your grandparents wore. Today, dentures are more modern, natural looking, and effective, letting people who wear them smile and eat with confidence. They are also comfortable and fitted specifically to your mouth, to allow for an easy adjustment period and to reduce the chances of discomfort along the gums. At Ceramco Dental Clinic,we manufacture your Dentures In-House, although the treatment phase spans over 5 appointments, they can be completed in 2 appointments. Ceramco’s Prosthodontist conducts Home visits for Ambulatory patients. Different types of dentures are available at our clinic– complete and partial dentures. Complete dentures are used when all the teeth are missing, while partial dentures are used when some natural teeth remain. We even repair dentures as well as do emergency dental treatment, which having an In-house lab makes this truly possible.
