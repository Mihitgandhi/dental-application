Our Services

Dental Implant

It is a titanium post surgically placed into the jawbone to support a replacement tooth. It provides a durable and natural-looking solution for missing teeth, restoring oral function and aesthetics.

Teeth Whitening

Also known as bleaching of teeth is a cosmetic procedure of whitening the teeth. People with stained or dull teeth usually benefit from whitening which is a safe and effective way to brighten discoloured teeth.

Tooth Removal

Dental extraction is removal of a tooth from mouth. Wisdom teeth (third molars) are last teeth to appear in your mouth, occurring normally 17 and 25 years of age, a time of life called the “Age of Wisdom”.
