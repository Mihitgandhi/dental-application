Dr. Abhishek Shah

Dr Abhishek M Shah (MDS -Oral & Maxillofacial Surgeon) is the Owner, MD & Principal dentist at MaxSurge Dental Clinic.

He started his bright academic career with a graduate degree of Bachelor of Dental Surgery from Karnataka University. Holding on to his urge to specialize, he aquired the Master of Dental Surgery in Orthodontics and Dentofacial Orthopaedics from the same University with First University rank amongst all MDS COURSES & Gold Medal.

He has a well equipped dental clinic with the latest instrument and the infrastructure is one of the best in the city. He treats his patients with exceptional professional expertise and in an extremely friendly manner. He offers both basic & advanced dental treatment with latest technology and excellent skill.

He has undergone numerous advanced training sessions to further sharpen his professional skills. He regularly attends seminars, symposia, workshops, programmes and conferences on different topics to be updated of latest developments in his speciality field.
